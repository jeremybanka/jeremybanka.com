DOLORES

My first typographic experiment, Dolores was an attempt to construct a monospaced pixel typeface within absurdly tiny metrics. The styles of Dolores are named after the height of its lowercase x in pixels.

Dolores Five: Totally readable. Some stylistic inflection.

Dolores Three: Readable, though characters are more abstract.

Dolores Two: Some characters are identical, but readable if you try.
