DIRECTIVE

an authoritative anachronism

Directive is an experimental take on the Roman Capitalis. Designed fairly light and with extremely high, but low severity vertical contrast, the design contradicts the diagonal axis of the original carvings. I mostly use Directive for drop capitals.
