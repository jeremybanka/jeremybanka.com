Logotype

I designed this logotype in Glyphs for my longtime card game project.

These were my first attempts to give my game a custom logotype.

Wayfarer was originally called DELVE. I chose this name because it was monosyllabic, uncommon, and five letters long, which creates pleasing spacing arrangements. When I learned in 2017 that this name was already in use by a board game with similar gameplay themes, I decided to rebrand.

Arena, Delve, and Quest are the three types of games you can play with the Wayfarer system.

I decided to keep the DELVE name and 2016 logo design for one of Wayfarer's three ‘Formats.’ This way, the assets could be used to differentiate parts of the content structure I developed and show the scope of this project.
