BATTLE GUIDE



Overview

The Wayfarer Battle System simulates a dangerous encounter between two or more characters in the Wayfarer universe. Within the Battle System, characters, the items they wear and carry, and the special actions they perform are represented by game cards. Using this system, players can team up to fight monsters, or square off against each other.
